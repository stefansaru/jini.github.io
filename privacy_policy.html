Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. The app does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information, and does not transmit any gameplay data to our servers.

All progress (completed puzzles, settings, hint counts) is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage and is never sent anywhere.

Voluntary defect reporting. If you choose to report a technical issue, you may send an email to hello@jini.solutions. To help us investigate and resolve the issue, we recommend including some or all of the following — the choice of what to share is entirely yours:

  • Phone model
  • Phone operating system version
  • Your Amethyst version and in-app settings
  • Error logs captured by the app
  • Screenshots or screen recordings showing the malfunction

This information is used solely to diagnose and resolve the reported defect. It is stored on Google Workspace email servers and is not shared with any third party. You may request deletion of the email thread at any time by contacting us at hello@jini.solutions; please be aware that deletion is carried out immediately upon request but will permanently remove the correspondence and may prevent us from completing the investigation.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising network operated by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID / GAID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device model, OS version, language, and time zone

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

Legal basis (EU/EEA/UK users): processing by AdMob is based on your explicit consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). On first launch you will be shown a consent dialogue powered by Google's User Messaging Platform. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time from Settings → Privacy → Manage ad consent.

If you decline personalised advertising, the app will display non-personalised ads instead and all features remain fully available.

Google Play Services

The app uses Google Play Services for standard Android functionality. Google Play Services may collect certain device and usage information as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at hello@jini.solutions and we will take prompt action to remove it.

Data retention

Because we do not collect personal data ourselves, there is no data held by us to retain or delete. Game progress stored on your device is deleted when you uninstall the app. For data held by Google (AdMob / Play Services), refer to Google's data retention policies.

Your rights (EU / EEA / UK — GDPR)

If you are located in the EU, EEA, or UK, you have the following rights regarding any personal data processed in connection with your use of this app:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — request confirmation of whether your data is being processed and obtain a copy.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3)) — withdraw advertising consent at any time via Settings → Privacy → Manage ad consent. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — you may lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority in your country of residence. For Romania: Autoritatea Națională de Supraveghere a Prelucrării Datelor cu Caracter Personal (ANSPDCP).

To exercise your rights, contact us at hello@jini.solutions. We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days and respond substantively within 30 calendar days.

Because we do not hold personal data ourselves (see above), most requests relate to data held by Google/AdMob. Where applicable, we will forward your request to Google using their User Data Deletion tools.

Your rights (California — CCPA)

California residents may opt out of the sharing of personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising. To do so:

  • Use the in-app consent control: Settings → Privacy → Manage ad consent (select non-personalised ads).
  • Adjust your device-level ad preferences via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.
  • Manage Google ad personalisation at adssettings.google.com.

We do not discriminate against users who exercise their CCPA rights. All app features are equally available regardless of ad consent choice.
