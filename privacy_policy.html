Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-03 20:49 UTC

Amethyst ("we", "our", "the app") is a free word-puzzle game. This page explains what data is collected when you use the app and how it is used.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. The app does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information, and does not transmit any gameplay data to our servers.

All progress (completed puzzles, settings, hint counts) is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage and is never sent anywhere.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising network operated by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device model, OS version, language, and time zone

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

On first launch you will be shown a consent dialogue (powered by Google's User Messaging Platform) that lets you choose whether ads are personalised or non-personalised. You can change your choice at any time from the in-app settings screen.

Google Play Services

The app uses Google Play Services for standard Android functionality. Google Play Services may collect certain device and usage information as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at hello@jini.solutions and we will take prompt action to remove it.

Data retention

Because we do not collect personal data ourselves, there is no data held by us to retain or delete. For data held by Google (AdMob / Play Services), refer to Google's data retention policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@jini.solutions
