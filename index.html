About

Amethyst is a free mobile word-puzzle game in the 4 Pics 1 Word genre. Look at four images, figure out the single word they all share, and tap the letters from the bank to spell it out.

We respect your privacy. The app does not require an account and stores all progress locally on your device. Read our Privacy Policy.
